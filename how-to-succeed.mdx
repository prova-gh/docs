## Mindset and Approach

### Take it Seriously
We acknowledge that our AI isn't perfect, but if you take the assessment seriously, it will help you demonstrate your understanding effectively. Using AI allows us to give every student the opportunity to show their knowledge and learning progress in an interactive way.

### Be Clear and Concise
- **Articulate your answers clearly**: Speak in a clear, professional manner
- **Avoid long pauses**: Try not to take extended pauses during your responses, otherwise the AI may interrupt you
- **Stay focused**: Keep your responses relevant and on-topic

## During the Assessment

- **Think out loud**: Share your thought process as you work through problems
- **Ask clarifying questions**: If something isn't clear, don't hesitate to ask
- **Be authentic**: Let your genuine understanding and approach shine through
- **Stay engaged**: Treat the AI assessor as you would an instructor or teaching assistant

## Remember

This is your opportunity to demonstrate your understanding and knowledge in an interactive way. The AI is designed to understand how you think and approach academic challenges, so focus on explaining your reasoning and methodology as you work through each problem.
