We're not your typical assessment. Instead of generic questions, you'll work through real scenarios that help you demonstrate your understanding and knowledge in interactive ways. 

Our AI will ask follow-up questions to understand how you think through problems, reason through concepts, and approach academic challenges. It's not just about what you know, it's about how you think and why you approach learning the way you do. 

## Enhancing Learning Assessment

Prova is designed to help you as a student demonstrate your understanding through interactive learning experiences. We're enhancing educational assessment by giving every student the opportunity to show their knowledge and learning progress in ways that go beyond traditional testing methods.

## Real-World Scenarios

Rather than traditional test questions, our assessments present you with realistic academic scenarios. This allows you to:

- Demonstrate your understanding and reasoning
- Show how you approach complex academic problems
- Reveal your thought process and learning approach
- Showcase your communication and explanation skills

## Interactive Assessment Experience

Our AI assessor adapts to your responses, creating a dynamic conversation that explores not just what you know, but how you understand it. This interactive approach provides a more comprehensive view of your learning and academic capabilities.
